# Employee Handbook

## Leave Policy

Full-time employees receive twenty days of paid annual leave each calendar year. Leave requests should be submitted at least ten business days before the planned absence unless the absence is urgent.

## Security

Confidential company documents must not be shared outside the organization. Security incidents should be reported to the security team within one business day.

## Remote Work

Employees may work remotely with manager approval. Teams should document recurring operating procedures in the internal knowledge base.
